Emerging Tech Presentation

October 10, 2016

Slides from a short presentation I gave on the emerging technology of Interactive Architecture. Emergent technologies are opening new avenues for interactivity in the architectural design process. Through the implementation of new technologies architecture can respond to its immediate context, be that weather or environmental response or user response. Interactivity in architecture allow for the real-time interface between the user and the architecture creating new dimensions of human experience and relationship with the built environment.
