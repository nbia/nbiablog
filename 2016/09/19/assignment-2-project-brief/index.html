Assignment 2

Mapping the UnMappable

Notations are “abstract machines” capable of producing new configurations out of given materials. They work across gaps of time and space but are not universal.

Stan Alan-Points + Lines: Diagrams and Projects for the City

Reading:

mapping-the-unmappable

This assignment will allow you to create an environment in the Unity game engine that begins to articulate the process/events in the city that we cannot understand or see. The ability of immersive tools to create environments that articulate new visual forms of notation is a tremendous asset for designers.

You are to create an environment that best articulates an abstract experience (in the city) and develop a narrative that expresses a situation that we cannot experience in the physical world.

Elements to consider in development:

-Sound

-Movement

-Texture

-Form

Project Due Oct. 10, 2016
